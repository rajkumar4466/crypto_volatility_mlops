    - 2 private subnets (10.0.3.0/24, 10.0.4.0/24) in different AZs
    - Public route table with 0.0.0.0/0 -> IGW, associated with public subnets

      - `rds_sg`: inbound 5432 from airflow_sg only
      - `redis_sg`: inbound 6379 from lambda_sg and airflow_sg
      - `lambda_sg`: outbound 6379 to redis_sg, outbound 443 to 0.0.0.0/0
    - Variables: project_name, aws_region
    - Outputs: vpc_id, public_subnet_ids, private_subnet_ids, airflow_sg_id, rds_sg_id, redis_sg_id, lambda_sg_id
